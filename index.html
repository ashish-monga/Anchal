About the Studio

We are a proudly female-led design house with a large showroom and full in-house manufacturing. For 40 years we've created jewellery for clients in UK, Dubai, London, USA, Punjab and Delhi. Our process blends artisanal techniques and modern CAD to produce pieces that are joyful to wear.
